About HACK 3.0 2019

Hack3.0 will be successor over the previous hackathon organized by CSEC. It will be organized on 9th and 10th of Feb 2019. It will involve people working in teams over a period of two days to try to create a project that can be used by end users. We welcome as much participation from the college as possible.

View Schedule

Why HACK 3.0?

Hack 3.0 will be a perfect opportunity for those seeking to apply their knowledge to hands-on projects and for those eager to venture into new areas of computing and learn new skills. It will give you an opportunity to brainstorm ingenious ideas and implement them into real-life projects. Being a team event, it will help you gain experience of teamwork and allow you to showcase your potential while competing with the best of minds.

Who Should Attend?

Students from all branches who have a knack for learning outside the classrooms are equally welcome to participate in the hackathon.

The inexperienced students who wish to participate will be provided with mentors to guide them and to seek help from throughout their project.

What People Ask

How it works?
People from final year have graciously agreed to mentor people from the third, second and first year (if needed). Of course, you still have the choice of doing it alone, but please make sure your team satisfies all the requirements given below.
What is minimum size of team?
4
What is maximum size of team?
5
What is maximum size of student from third year which can be in same team?
2. And then you have to take rest of members from first year.
What is maximum size of student from second year which can be in same team?
3.You can then select rest of your members from first year.
What if I don't have any team?
Apply as individual, we will make your team which will be a balanced team.
What do I make?
There is no theme as such, but stuff that helps make people’s lives easier in the college would be encouraged.
Can I participate remotely ?
No, you have to physically present at the venue of hackathon. :-)
